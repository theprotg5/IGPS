About Our School

Indira Gandhi Public School was established in 1993 by Mr. Avdhesh Rajpurohit with a vision to provide quality education to children in Prempura, Bhopal. Our school is affiliated with the MP Board and offers classes from Nursery to 8th Grade.

We focus on creating a balanced learning environment that nurtures academic excellence, physical development, and moral values. Our mission is to empower every students to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

Our Academics

Curriculum

We follow the MP Board curriculum with additional focus on practical learning and skill development. Our approach combines traditional teaching methods with modern educational techniques.

  • Comprehensive MP Board syllabus
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Digital learning resources

Classes Offered

We provide education from Nursery to 8th Grade with age-appropriate teaching methodologies and learning environments for each level.

  • Pre-Primary: Nursery, LKG, UKG
  • Primary: Classes 1 to 5
  • Middle School: Classes 6 to 8

Extracurricular

We believe in holistic development and offer various extracurricular activities to nurture talents and build confidence in our students.

Admission Process

How to Apply

  1. 1

    Collect Application Form

    Visit our school office to collect the application form.

  2. 2

    Submit Completed Form

    Return the filled form along with required documents.

  3. 3

    Entrance Assessment

    Students may be required to take an assessment test.

  4. 4

    Interview

    Parents and students will be called for an interview.

  5. 5

    Confirmation

    Selected students will receive admission confirmation.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Address proof
  • Aadhar card of student and parents
